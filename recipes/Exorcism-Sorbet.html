Exorcism Sorbet

Description

This dish was prepared by accident by Senshi and the rest of the party in the process of dispelling ghosts. A chilled version of the fourth recipe, Special Interfaith Holy Water, the sorbet is a sweet, refreshing treat that melts on the tongue.

Ingredients

  • Special Interfaith Holy Water
  • Sturdy jar
  • Sacred cordon rope
  • Ghosts
